I'm Karina Arengato

Thank you for checking out my oil painting portfolio. Each of my art pieces has been a product of pure inspiration from my deep love of outer space. The featured paintings in this collection were an experience and experiment of creation with the tools and media available to me at the time. They are unnamed and are open to interpretation.

I invite you to explore these works and let them speak to you stirring up any thoughts or feelings, and welcome any comments or story you may wish to share via the contact form. Originals are available for purchase where available.
